Motivation towards problem... of waste segregation

Huge amount of waste is generated around billions of tons every year but only 30% of waste is recycled. Waste management has become an urgent need to increase the percentage of waste recycling and reusing. "Segregation of waste" becomes the first step in waste management. The existing system employs manual labour to segregate dry waste into metal,plastic and paper. It is less efficient and a tedious task to separate the amount of generated waste. Segregating the waste at the source of generation solves the crux of the problem of waste management.

Proposed Solution

The Solution has been designed for effective automatic segregation of waste at the source itself, thereby reducing the physical efforts. The system is based on concepts of Deep Learning,a subset of artificial intelligence, Image processing, Computer vision and Internet of Things (IoT).

Methodology

Segregation of waste includes two steps:

Identification : the trash is perfectly identified by the image processing and CNN(convolutional neural network) algorithms.
Separation : The trash placed on the main flap according to the result from algorithm falls into respective compartment of the bin.
